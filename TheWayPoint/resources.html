Examination of Conscience

Preparing for the Sacrament of Confession can be challenging, but a thorough Examination of Conscience can help you approach it with a prepared and sincere heart. This tool allows you to select specific areas where you seek forgiveness and generates a printable PDF that you can bring to Confession.
