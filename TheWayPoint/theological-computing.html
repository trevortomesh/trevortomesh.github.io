Welcome to Theological Computing

Theological Computing is an interdisciplinary field that integrates technology and faith, offering new ways to explore and understand spiritual concepts. This page will serve as a hub for resources, tools, and insights into how technology can deepen theological engagement.

Featured Projects

The Divinity Engine

The Divinity Engine is a computational tool designed to simulate and model theological principles, including virtues, sins, and moral decision-making. By leveraging technology, it provides a platform for ethical reflection and spiritual exploration.

AI for Intercessory Prayer

Exploring the potential of AI to act as a medium for intercessory prayer, this project incorporates Catholic theological principles to create meaningful digital engagement with the communion of saints.
