What These Are

OmegaChain coins are unique, non-financial digital challenge coins. They are commemorative keepsakes, not cryptocurrency, NFTs, securities, or investments.

Why I Make Them

I designed these coins as a personal way to thank people who support my work, including Ko-fi donors, and to mark that appreciation with something verifiable.

How They Verify

Each PNG contains hidden verification data. This page checks it against a public JSON registry, issuer signature, image hash, and append-only hash chain.
